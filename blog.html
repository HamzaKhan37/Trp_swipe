A Bangalore to Chikmagalur Road trip

Are you interested in visiting a picturesque hill station near Bangalore? What could be more beautiful than Chikmagalur’s magnificent gateway? It’s a terrific area to visit on weekends as a vacation from Bangalore. There are many trails to get to this location, but we’ll take a road trip from Bangalore to Chikmagalur today. A quick travel plan can spice up your everyday routine.

What can be the best time to visit Bangalore to Chikmagalur?

Chikmagalur is a beautiful place to visit at any time of the year. However, having a fun trip to this destination from September to March is far more exciting. The monsoon has just disappeared, the streams are filled, the lakes and waterfalls are magical to witness live. The temperature is around 20 degrees celsius.

How to get from Bangalore to Chikmagalur? – The Route

To drive through the three unique ways from Bangalore to Chikmagalur needs a special mention. There are three routes by road that you can take to get into Chickmaglur from Bangalore. Here we have mentioned it for you.

Route 1: The Arsikere Route

You are required to take the road route from NH75 via NH 73 that is around 265 kilometers. It will take you only 5 hours to reach Chikmagalur.

Bangalore to Kunigal to Yediyur to Dasarighatta to Arsikere to Chikmagalur.

There are exciting places that you require to explore. Those are;

  • Begur Lake: There are 15 varieties of birds that you must see to believe the fact of how magnificent nature’s creation is!
  • Sri Chowdeshwari Temple: An ancient Hindu temple is located in Dasarighatta at Tiptur. An anthill is the most happening thing here that you must explore. The idol is there.
  • Manjarabad fort: In Donegal, you’ll find another defense which is 3240 feet high. It has unique star shapes on it. This is a good option for a short trek.
    • Route 2: The Belur Route 

    To take the Belur route, you need to take the road map that covers 225 kilometers the following places;

    Bangalore to Yediyur to Channarayapatna to Hassan to Belur to Chikmagalur.

    While taking this route, you must stop by and witness the beauty of the places mentioned below:

  • Yediyur Lake: You can stop by and spend a few hours at this gorgeous lake. Try the scooter rides and boating to enjoy the serenity of nature.
  • Gorur Dam: Have you seen the tallest reservoir? The Gorur Dam is the tallest reservoir in Karnataka, which resides at the height of 146 feet above sea level.
  • Shettihalli Church: It is an impressive sight to witness the Drowning church at Shettihalli. In monsoon, it is submerged with rainwater. Yet, the ruins of the church are a must to see.
